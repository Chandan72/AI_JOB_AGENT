## Usage Notes
- **Version A** is tailored to a recruiter’s need for role fit and required experience (8 years, client-facing). It leads with the hard requirement and a clear, relevant metric.
- **Version B** is tailored to a hiring manager’s strategic focus on team impact and results. It leads with the operational outcome and offers portfolio proof.
- **Before sending:** Replace bracketed details `[ ]` with the specific recruiter/hiring manager name if found. If not, use a respectful generic like "Hi there" but a name is always better.
- **Wait 5 business days** after sending Version A before sending Version B to the hiring manager, if their contact is found. Never send both on the same day.
